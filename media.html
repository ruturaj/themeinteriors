A few words About Us

The Founder and Chief Designer

Theme Interiors was founded in the year 2000 at Trivandrum. The Founder and Chief Designer is Mrs. Veena Dhekane. She has lead the team for the last 14 years and designed over 100 homes.

A team of skilled worksmen
The team of carpenters and polishers have continuously challenged themselves by taking up innovative and tough design projects. The team deliver creative designs and sturdy interiors in time and with precise craftsmanship.

Theme Interiors has partnered with many accessory showrooms across Kerala to procure high quality fixtures, glass and upholstrey. Theme Interiors' experience ensures that you get the best selection to make your home beautiful.
